About SOF IGKO Exam

Calling all curious minds in Grades 1-10! Are you ready to put your general knowledge to the test? Join the exciting world of SOF IGKO (International General Knowledge Olympiad) and embark on a journey of discovery!


Why participate in SOF IGKO?

  • Expand your horizons: Explore fascinating topics from science and history to current affairs and the arts.
  • Challenge yourself: Test your knowledge and discover new strengths.
  • Boost your confidence: Gain recognition for your achievements and build self-esteem.
  • Win exciting prizes: Top performers stand a chance to win medals, certificates, and other exciting rewards.
  • Benchmark your learning: See how you compare with students across the country and globally.

What to expect

  • Curriculum-aligned questions: The IGKO syllabus is designed to complement your school curriculum.
  • Age-appropriate difficulty levels: Challenges designed specifically for each grade, ensuring a fair and engaging experience.
  • Stimulating question formats: Multiple-choice questions that encourage critical thinking and problem-solving.
  • A fun and enriching experience: IGKO is more than just a test; it's a chance to learn, grow, and have fun!

How to register

  • Visit https://sofworld.org/igko/about-igko-exam/how-to-participate or contact your school coordinator for more information.
